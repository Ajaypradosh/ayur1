The Magic of Cardamom

Cardamom is the Queen of Spices. Yet, this wonder spice does more than just add flavor to food and beverages. It also has excellent and multiple medicinal properties, is used in perfumes, is chewed as a mouth freshener, and makes for a very thoughtful gift.

Cardamom is known for its sweet aroma and is often used in desserts and beverages.

Rich in antioxidants, cardamom helps in digestion and improves oral health.

Used in traditional medicine, cardamom may help reduce blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Graded to your Requirement

‘One size doesn’t fit all’ is true in the case of cardamom as well.
Because of their multiple uses and benefits, cardamom pods of different sizes are used in different applications.
